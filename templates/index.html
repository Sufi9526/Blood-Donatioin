Giving blood is simple and it saves lives. When you give blood, it is collected so it can be used to treat someone else.

Before You Give Blood

  • Check if you are eligible to donate blood.
  • Register your interest or call 0300 123 23 23.
  • Log in to your online account and book an appointment.
  • Follow the guidelines to prepare for blood donation.

When You Give Blood

Welcome and Preparation

Bring your completed safety form and read the donor booklet.

Health Screening

  • Identity verification and health checkup.
  • Iron level (hemoglobin) test.
  • If unable to donate, staff will advise you accordingly.

Your Blood Donation

  • A needle is inserted into your vein to collect blood.
  • No pain should be felt, inform staff if any discomfort.
  • Blood donation takes around 5-10 minutes.
